About Us

Overland Trails is a Tasmanian Adventure company, offering all-inclusive packages that provide our customers with everything they need to experience this exciting activity regardless of experience.

Our company was established in 2023 and is based in North-West Tasmania. We offer access to some of Tasmania's best mountain biking trails which are world-class in design. We also offer exclusive access to many private trails found on the Overland Trails property which are designed and maintained by us.

Regardless of your experience level, we have something to offer you. Our experienced coaches can help you to learn the basics through to advanced techniques. We can provide transfers, accomodation and meals so that you can focus on getting as much time on track as possible.

Packages

Package 1 Package 2

4 Day Adventure

Our first package has everything you'll need for your Mountain Biking Adventure.

It includes transfers, accomodation and 4 day bike hire, as well as your very own guide who will tailor your itinerary to your personal tastes!

Also included are all meals, sourced from local Tasmanian produce with a menu produced in conjunction with some of Tasmania's local Resaurants.

2 Day Adventure

Our second package caters towards more experienced Mountain Bikers, with bike hire (duel suspension), accomodation and meals.

At the start of your first day, you will be given a quick tour of all accessible trails to help you get started.

Also included are all meals, sourced from local Tasmanian produce with a menu produced in conjunction with some of Tasmania's local Resaurants.
